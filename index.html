Hi there! I'm Ethan.

Welcome to "Open and Closed Media", an interactive web exploration.

I am your tour guide, and together we will explore two types of social media. We will look at them through the lens of two apps that I believe represent them quite well.


Yes!

The two apps are Discord and Instagram.

Although they are what I am most familiar with, these two apps can represent many other social media apps quite well.

In this web experience, you'll join me in exploring both apps and the media type they represent. We will discuss how they work, their key features, significant issues, and ways to become healthy users of both.

Along the way, there will be spots for your input. This will help you understand our journey on a more personal level.
